EduTwin is a next-generation academic registry portal that enables students to self-enroll, select their institution, and track grade records with absolute transparency.

Enter Portal

Core Features

Multi-College Roster

Colleges act as independent registries. Roll numbers are isolated by college, resolving verification collisions completely.

Student Self-Enrollment

Students register details, majors, and link to their specific institution directly. Administrators then update and verify marksheets.

Live Record Fetching

Dynamic autocomplete validation. Selecting your college and roll number fetches name information on-the-fly before accessing marksheets.

How to Use the Portal

For Institutions

  • 1

    Register an administrator account representing your college / university.

  • 2

    View self-registered student candidates or manually enroll student identifiers.

  • 3

    Record marks for subjects to compute aggregate percentages and grades instantly.

For Students

  • 1

    Click "Enter Portal" and navigate to the Student self-registration section.

  • 2

    Submit your name, roll number, department, and choose your college.

  • 3

    Use the Verification login dashboard to instantly pull your mark sheets.
